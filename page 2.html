Speaking of skin let's get more in depth. Our skin is one of our most important organs that requires much care.

FACIAL CARE

Facial care varies with each person based on their skin type. There are 5 kinds of skin types.

  • Oily - shiny / greasy appearance , enlarged pores
  • Dry - flaky, rough patches on face & body
  • Combo - drier in some parts , oily in others
  • Sensitive - redness, prone to itchiness
  • Normal - balance, non-sensitive, clear
